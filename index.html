About Me

Hello,

My name is Collin Reich. I am a senior Computer Science student at the University of Delaware with a minor in Entrepreneurship and concentration in Cyber Security. I am from Long Island, NY, and I plan on moving to the Bay Area in California after graduation in 2022. On campus I have held several positions as both a teaching assistant and lab assistant for a variety of computer science courses. I also currently serve as the Internal Professional Development Chair for the Alpha Kappa Psi professional business fraternity.

This website was created as a professional tool and resource, as well as an opportunity to reflect my enthusiasm for web development. Several areas of web development I am particularly interested in are web security, web services, as well as UI/UX design. In my career, my goal is to become a highly skilled full-stack engineer, and eventually a project manager after earning my MBA. I am also highly interested in pursuing my own entrepreneurial endeavors, after gaining adequate industry experience.

You can download my resume above to take a look at my technical skills, professional experience, and relevant courses I have taken. Below you will find information about several projects I have worked on, as well as my contact information. Feel free to contact me at any time. Enjoy!
